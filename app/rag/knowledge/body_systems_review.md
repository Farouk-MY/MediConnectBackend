# Body Systems Review — Systematic Intake Questions by Organ System

## Cardiovascular System (Heart & Blood Vessels)

### Symptoms to Screen For
- Chest pain or discomfort
- Shortness of breath (at rest or with exertion)
- Palpitations (racing, skipping, irregular heartbeat)
- Leg swelling (edema)
- Dizziness or fainting
- Exercise intolerance (getting tired easily with activity)

### Key Follow-up Questions
- Does the pain relate to physical activity?
- Do you wake up short of breath at night?
- Do your ankles swell by evening?
- Family history of heart disease?

## Respiratory System (Lungs & Airways)

### Symptoms to Screen For
- Cough (dry or productive)
- Shortness of breath
- Wheezing
- Chest tightness
- Hemoptysis (coughing blood)
- Snoring / sleep apnea symptoms

### Key Follow-up Questions
- Do you smoke or have you smoked? How many years? How many per day?
- Exposure to dust, chemicals, or fumes at work?
- Seasonal pattern to symptoms?
- Can you climb stairs without stopping?

## Gastrointestinal System (Digestive)

### Symptoms to Screen For
- Abdominal pain (location, character, timing)
- Nausea and vomiting
- Diarrhea or constipation
- Heartburn / acid reflux
- Difficulty swallowing
- Blood in stool (red or black/tarry)
- Appetite and weight changes
- Bloating

### Key Follow-up Questions
- Regular bowel movements? Changes recently?
- Relation to specific foods?
- Alcohol consumption?
- Recent travel? (parasites, traveler's diarrhea)

## Neurological System (Brain & Nerves)

### Symptoms to Screen For
- Headaches (type, frequency, severity)
- Dizziness or vertigo
- Numbness or tingling
- Weakness in limbs
- Seizures or convulsions
- Vision changes
- Memory or concentration problems
- Balance and coordination issues

### Key Follow-up Questions
- Sudden onset or gradual?
- One side of body or both?
- Associated with speech or vision changes? (stroke screening)
- Recent head injury?

## Musculoskeletal System (Bones, Joints, Muscles)

### Symptoms to Screen For
- Joint pain (which joints? one or multiple?)
- Muscle pain or stiffness
- Back pain (upper, lower, radiation)
- Joint swelling, redness, warmth
- Limited range of motion
- Morning stiffness (how long?)
- History of fractures or injuries

### Key Follow-up Questions
- Activity-related or at rest?
- Morning stiffness lasting more than 30 minutes? (suggests inflammatory)
- Recent injury or fall?
- Difficulty with daily activities (dressing, walking, climbing stairs)?

## Genitourinary System

### Symptoms to Screen For
- Urinary frequency, urgency
- Pain or burning during urination
- Blood in urine
- Urinary incontinence
- Flank or lower back pain
- Sexual health concerns

### Key Follow-up Questions
- How many times per day/night?
- Color of urine?
- Fever with urinary symptoms? (suggests infection spreading)
- Previous kidney stones or UTIs?

## Endocrine System (Hormones)

### Symptoms to Screen For
- Unexplained weight changes (gain or loss)
- Excessive thirst or hunger
- Temperature intolerance (hot or cold)
- Fatigue without clear cause
- Changes in hair, skin, nails
- Menstrual irregularities (women)
- Excessive sweating

### Key Follow-up Questions
- Thyroid conditions in family?
- Blood sugar ever tested?
- Changes in energy levels?

## Dermatological (Skin)

### Symptoms to Screen For
- Rashes (location, appearance, duration)
- Itching (localized or generalized)
- New or changing moles
- Skin lesions or wounds not healing
- Hair loss
- Nail changes
- Bruising easily

### Key Follow-up Questions
- New soaps, detergents, or products?
- Previous skin conditions?
- Sun exposure habits?
- Contact with plants, animals, or chemicals?

## ENT (Ear, Nose, Throat)

### Symptoms to Screen For
- Sore throat
- Ear pain or discharge
- Hearing loss or tinnitus (ringing)
- Nasal congestion or discharge
- Nosebleeds
- Hoarseness
- Difficulty swallowing
- Jaw pain

### Key Follow-up Questions
- One ear or both?
- Associated with cold symptoms?
- Exposure to loud noise?
- Allergies?

## Ophthalmological (Eyes)

### Symptoms to Screen For
- Vision changes (blurry, double, loss)
- Eye pain or redness
- Discharge from eyes
- Floaters or flashes of light
- Sensitivity to light
- Dry eyes or excessive tearing

### Key Follow-up Questions
- Both eyes or one?
- Sudden or gradual onset?
- Wear glasses or contacts?
- Last eye exam?

## Psychiatric / Mental Health

### Symptoms to Screen For (approach sensitively)
- Mood changes (persistent sadness, irritability)
- Anxiety or excessive worry
- Sleep disturbances (insomnia, hypersomnia)
- Appetite changes
- Concentration difficulties
- Social withdrawal
- Substance use changes

### Key Follow-up Questions (gentle approach)
- How has your mood been lately?
- Are you sleeping well?
- Anything causing you particular stress?
- Important: Screen for safety (suicidal ideation) when clinically appropriate
